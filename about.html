About

Most of my days revolve around OpenTelemetry, Prometheus, and making sure metrics and traces actually work at scale across a lot of Kubernetes clusters. I'm a triager on the OpenTelemetry Collector Contrib project, where I review issues and PRs and maintain telemetrygen. I also contribute to other parts of the OTel ecosystem, kube-prometheus-stack, and Cortex.

Outside of work, I enjoy speaking at conferences and sharing what I've learned along the way. I also have an Airedale Terrier named Terry who occasionally makes it into my slides.
